# 📦 Strategic Supply Chain Analytics

> **An end-to-end Supply Chain Analytics project using Python, SQL Server, and Power BI to uncover business insights from 180K+ supply chain transactions.**

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Supply chain organizations generate large volumes of operational data, making it difficult to identify the factors affecting profitability, customer retention, delivery performance, and revenue leakage. This project analyzes a global supply chain dataset to uncover actionable insights that support data-driven operational and strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and repeat customer trends.
- Evaluate sales, profitability, and product performance.
- Identify high-performing and underperforming markets.
- Assess delivery efficiency and operational bottlenecks.
- Detect revenue leakage and fraud exposure.
- Build interactive dashboards for business decision-makers.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- **Repeat customers** generate the majority of business revenue, highlighting the importance of **customer retention strategies**.
- Nearly **50% of total revenue** is contributed by the **top 20% of customers**, indicating a **highly concentrated customer base**.
- **Europe** and **LATAM** generate the highest revenue but also experience the **highest delivery delays**, which may reduce **customer satisfaction** and **repeat purchases**.
- **Western Europe, Central America, and South America** show **untapped revenue potential** despite having a sizable customer base.
- **Higher discount levels** significantly reduce **profit margins** without increasing average sales, indicating an **inefficient discount strategy**.
- **Fishing, Cleats, and Camping & Hiking** are the **highest revenue-generating product categories** and should remain inventory priorities.
- A considerable share of **revenue** remains tied to **pending, cancelled, and fraud-risk orders**, creating opportunities to improve **order completion** and **cash flow**.
- **Bank Transfer transactions** contribute the highest **fraud exposure**, highlighting the need for stronger **payment verification**.
- **Delivery delays** across several product categories may increase **cancellation risk** and negatively impact **long-term customer loyalty**.
- Revenue is heavily concentrated among a relatively small group of **high-value customers**, emphasizing the importance of **personalized retention programs**.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Strengthen **customer loyalty programs** and **personalized engagement** to improve **repeat customer retention**.
- Optimize **logistics operations** in **Europe** and **LATAM** to reduce **delivery delays** and improve **customer satisfaction**.
- Replace **broad discounting** with **targeted promotional campaigns** to improve **profitability**.
- Increase **customer spending** in **underperforming regions** through **cross-selling** and **personalized marketing**.
- Enhance **payment verification** and **fraud detection** for **high-risk payment methods**.
- Prioritize **faster order fulfillment** to reduce **pending** and **cancelled orders**, improving **revenue realization**.
- Focus **inventory planning** and **marketing investments** on **high-performing product categories** to maximize business growth.
